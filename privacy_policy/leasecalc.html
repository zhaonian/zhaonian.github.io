Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 17, 2026

1. Overview

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how LeaseCalc ("we," "our") handles your information.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect personal data. All lease and finance calculations are performed locally on your device. We do not upload your deal data to any external servers.

3. Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (Google) to collect anonymous usage data such as feature interactions and session information. This data does not identify you personally and is used solely to improve the app experience.

4. Camera & Photo Library Access

The document scanning feature requires access to your device's camera and photo library. Images are processed on-device to extract deal fields and are not uploaded to any server.

5. Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymous identifiers (such as your App Store receipt) to validate your purchase status. This data is not linked to your personal identity.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
