Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 4, 2026

1. Overview

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how DualCap ("we," "our") handles your information.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect personal data. DualCap is designed to process your photos and videos locally on your device. We do not upload your media to any external servers.

3. Camera & Microphone Access

To function, the App requires access to your device's Camera and Microphone. This data is processed in real-time on your device solely for the purpose of capturing photos and videos. It is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

4. Photo Library Access

We require "Add Only" access to your Photo Library to save the videos and photos you capture. We do not read, scan, or upload your existing library content.

5. Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymous identifiers (such as your App Store receipt) to validate your purchase status. This data is not linked to your personal identity.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
