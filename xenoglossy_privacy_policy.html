Privacy Policy for Xenoglossy

Last updated: January 5, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: Xenoglossy is designed with privacy in mind. We process your text only to provide our transformation service and do not store, collect, or share your personal data.

1. Information We Process

Xenoglossy processes the following information to provide our text transformation service:

  • Selected Text: Text you select and choose to transform using our app
  • API Configuration: Your OpenAI and Gemini API keys (stored locally on your device)
  • App Preferences: Your selected tone and AI model preferences (stored locally)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Transforming your selected text according to your chosen tone
  • Communicating with AI services (OpenAI and Gemini) to process transformations
  • Remembering your app preferences and settings

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

When you use Xenoglossy to transform text, your selected text is sent to:

  • OpenAI (when using OpenAI models) - Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy
  • Google/Gemini (when using Gemini models) - Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
Important: We do not store, log, or have access to your text. It is sent directly from your device to the chosen AI service and the response is returned directly to you.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your text or personal data on our servers
  • Local Storage Only: API keys and preferences are stored locally on your Mac
  • Temporary Processing: Text is processed in memory only and not persisted
  • No Analytics: We do not collect usage analytics or tracking data

5. Permissions and Access

Xenoglossy requests the following macOS permissions:

  • Accessibility: To read selected text across applications and simulate keystrokes for pasting transformed text
  • Clipboard Access: To temporarily store transformed text for pasting

These permissions are used only for the core functionality of text transformation and are never used to collect or transmit data for other purposes.

6. Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • API keys are stored securely in your Mac's keychain
  • All communications with AI services use encrypted HTTPS connections
  • No persistent storage of your text content
  • App runs with minimal necessary permissions

7. Children's Privacy

Xenoglossy is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. International Users

By using Xenoglossy, you consent to the transfer of your text data to AI services that may be located outside your country of residence, including the United States.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Providing in-app notification for significant changes

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Stop using the service at any time
  • Delete the app and all associated data from your device
  • Contact us with questions about this privacy policy
  • Request information about data processing (though we do not retain your data)

11. Data Controllers

When you use Xenoglossy:

  • For app functionality: Zhaonian Luan (app developer) is the data controller
  • For AI processing: OpenAI and Google are independent data controllers for text sent to their services
